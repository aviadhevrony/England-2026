London — Day 4 🎭

10:00 → 14:00
⚡ Warner Bros. Studio Tour — The Making of Harry Potter 📍 Map ↗
Walk the actual sets — Great Hall, Diagon Alley, Platform 9¾, Hogwarts Express. Located in Leavesden, ~30 min from central London by train + shuttle. Book in advance — sells out fast. book.wbstudiotour.com ↗
14:00
🖼️ National Portrait Gallery — Marilyn Monroe exhibition 📍 Map ↗
Right next to Trafalgar Square. Beautifully designed gallery — always fabulous exhibitions. npg.org.uk ↗
Afternoon
🛍️ Shopping — Central London
Oxford Street 📍 Map ↗ — London's most famous high street, massive variety from H&M to flagship stores. Huge Selfridges department store at the west end.

Regent Street 📍 Map ↗ — more elegant than Oxford St, sweeping curved architecture. Hamleys (world's oldest toy shop), Liberty department store, Apple flagship.

Covent Garden 📍 Map ↗ — cobbled piazza with boutiques, Apple Market crafts & vintage, street performers. Great mix of unique finds and high street.

Carnaby Street 📍 Map ↗ — quirky independent shops, streetwear, bohemian vibe. A 2-min walk from Regent Street.

Bond Street / Mayfair 📍 Map ↗ — if you're feeling fancy: Chanel, Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Tiffany. The most upscale stretch in London.
