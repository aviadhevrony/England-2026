Good to know

Practical Info

💷
Money
Currency is British Pound (GBP). Cards and contactless/Apple Pay accepted everywhere. Worth having £50–100 cash for rural areas.
🌤️
Weather in August
Lake District: 14–20°C, can be rainy — pack layers and a waterproof jacket. London: 18–24°C, generally pleasant.
🚗
Getting Around
7-seater SUV for the whole trip. Drive on the left — narrow country roads in the Lakes and Dales.
🆘
Emergency
999 — police, fire, ambulance
111 — NHS non-emergency medical
112 — EU emergency (also works)
📱
Wi-Fi & SIM
Good 4G/5G coverage in cities and towns. Rural Lakes/Dales can have patchy signal. Download offline maps before you go.
