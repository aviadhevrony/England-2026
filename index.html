Good to know

Practical Info

💷
Money
Currency is British Pound (GBP). Cards and Apple Pay accepted everywhere. Worth having £50–100 cash for rural areas.
🌤️
Weather in August
Lake District: 14–20°C, can be rainy — pack layers and a waterproof. London: 18–24°C, generally pleasant.
🚗
Getting Around
7-seater SUV for the whole trip. Drive on the left — narrow country roads in the Lakes and Dales.
🆘
Emergency
999 — police, fire, ambulance
111 — NHS non-emergency medical
📱
Wi-Fi & SIM
Good 4G/5G in cities. Rural Lakes/Dales can be patchy — download offline maps before you go.
